Our Products

High-durability corrugated solutions engineered for industrial resilience. Available in various flute profiles and ply configurations.

3-Ply Boxes

Ideal for lightweight to medium items. Consists of a single fluted medium sandwiched between two liners for everyday shipping reliability.

5-Ply Boxes

Double-wall protection designed for heavy-duty industrial components. Offers superior stacking strength and puncture resistance.

7-Ply Boxes

Triple-wall construction for maximum protection. The benchmark for high-value exports and extremely heavy industrial machinery.

Flutes determine the thickness and strength of the box wall. A-Flute offers the best cushioning, B-Flute provides excellent crush resistance and foldability, C-Flute is the standard for versatile shipping, and E-Flute is a thin profile ideal for high-quality retail printing. We provide all flute configurations for your boxes as per your needs.
Our manufacturing process includes rigorous in-house testing, specifically Bursting Strength Tests (BST) and Edge Crush Tests (ECT), to guarantee your packaging withstands vertical stacking pressure and transit handling without failing.
